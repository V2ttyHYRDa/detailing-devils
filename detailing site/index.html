Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Professional-grade ceramic coating typically lasts 2-5 years depending on the product and maintenance. Our premium 9H ceramic coating offers 3-year durability with proper care. We recommend maintenance washes every 2-3 weeks and avoiding automated car washes to maximize longevity.

Graphene coating is the next evolution of ceramic coating. It offers superior water-spotting resistance, better heat dissipation, and enhanced durability. While ceramic coating provides excellent protection, graphene adds an extra layer of performance, especially in harsh weather conditions. The choice depends on your budget and protection needs.

Service duration varies: Premium Wash (1 hour), Interior Deep Clean (4 hours), Paint Enhancement (4-6 hours), Ceramic Coating (24 hours with curing time), PPF Installation (72 hours). We never rush the process – each vehicle receives meticulous attention to ensure perfection. We'll provide a loaner service or pickup/drop facility for longer jobs.

Avoid washing for 7 days after application to allow full curing. After that, use pH-neutral car shampoo and microfiber wash mitts. Wash in shade, never under direct sunlight. Avoid automated car washes and harsh chemicals. We provide a complimentary maintenance kit and detailed care instructions with every coating service.
